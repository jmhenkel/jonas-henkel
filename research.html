Research

Geometry, spectra, and computational methods.

My research lies in differential geometry, with a focus on homogeneous spaces, spectral questions, and the structure of geometric operators. I am also interested in computational and machine-learning-based approaches to geometric problems.

Spectral Geometry

Laplace spectra, Hodge Laplacians, and explicit spectral computations on homogeneous manifolds.

Homogeneous Spaces

Naturally reductive geometry, special geometric structures, and representation-theoretic methods.

AI and Computation

Symbolic computation with Python and SageMath, plus machine learning for non-linear geometric equations.

Computational Mathematics and Programming

Symbolic Computation

Extensive use of Python and SageMath to translate geometric and representation-theoretic problems into computational frameworks, including custom algebraic solvers.

Machine Learning

Worked with physics-informed neural networks on non-linear geometric equations, including the Nirenberg problem, and contributed to the AInstein project group at the London Geometry and Machine Learning Summer School.
