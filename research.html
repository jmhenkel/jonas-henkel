Research

My research lies in differential geometry, with a particular focus on spectral geometry, homogeneous spaces, and computational questions arising from geometric problems.

My dissertation project concerns explicit spectral questions for Laplace-type operators on homogeneous spaces and related bundles. Alongside this, I use symbolic computation with Python and SageMath to support calculations in differential geometry, and I am interested in how AI-based methods can support mathematical research.
