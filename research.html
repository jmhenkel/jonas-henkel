Research

My research lies in differential geometry, with a particular focus on spectral geometry, homogeneous spaces, and related computational questions.

Interests

My work concerns spectral geometry, homogeneous spaces, naturally reductive manifolds, and related questions in differential geometry. I am also interested in symbolic computation and in computational approaches to geometric problems.

Computation

I use Python and SageMath to translate geometric and representation-theoretic problems into computational frameworks, including custom symbolic calculations. I have also worked with physics-informed neural networks on non-linear geometric equations, including the Nirenberg problem.
