Jonas Henkel

I am a PhD student and research associate at Philipps-University of Marburg. My advisor is Prof. Dr. habil. Ilka Agricola.

My research interests include spectral geometry, homogeneous spaces, symbolic computation, and questions concerning the role of artificial intelligence in mathematical research.

Philipps-University of Marburg

Research

My current work focuses on spectral geometry on homogeneous spaces, especially explicit spectral questions related to Laplace-type operators and naturally reductive manifolds. I also work on computational approaches in geometry, including symbolic computation with Python and SageMath, and on the use of AI in mathematical practice.
