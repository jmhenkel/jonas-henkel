Jonas Henkel

I am a PhD student and research associate at Philipps-University of Marburg, working on spectral geometry on homogeneous spaces under the supervision of Prof. Dr. habil. Ilka Agricola.

My interests include spectral geometry, homogeneous spaces, symbolic computation, and questions concerning the role of artificial intelligence in mathematical research.

Research

My current research focuses on spectral geometry on homogeneous spaces, especially explicit spectral questions related to Laplace-type operators and naturally reductive manifolds. I also work on computational approaches in geometry, including symbolic computation with Python and SageMath, and on the use of AI in mathematical practice.
