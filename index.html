PhD Student in Mathematics

Spectral geometry, homogeneous spaces, and AI in mathematical research.

I am a PhD student and research associate at Philipps-University of Marburg, working on spectral geometry on homogeneous spaces under the supervision of Prof. Dr. habil. Ilka Agricola.

My research interests include naturally reductive geometry, Laplace spectra, symbolic computation, and the productive use of AI in mathematical practice.
