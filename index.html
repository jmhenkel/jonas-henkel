About

I am a PhD student and research associate at Philipps-University of Marburg. My advisor is Prof. Dr. habil. Ilka Agricola. I expect to defend my dissertation in July 2026.

My research interests include spectral geometry, homogeneous spaces, symbolic computation, and questions concerning the role of artificial intelligence in mathematical research.

Research

My current projects concern explicit spectral questions on homogeneous spaces, symbolic computation in differential geometry, and machine-learning-based approaches to geometric problems.
